The BMW 3 Seriesis a compact executive vehicle made by BMW. A German automaker since 1975.Its is the successor to the BMW 2002.

Available in Sedan, Coupe, Convertible and Grand Tourer.The German sedan is praised for its comfort, performance and everyday practicality. It competes with the Audi A4 and Mercedes C-class

History

BMW introduced the 3 series in 1975 as a replavement for the bmw 2002-a car that solidified BMW's reputation in the compact sedan category.The original e21 was a 2 door sedan and set the template which was carried on: RWD and driver-focused

The e30 expanded the range with the convertable, touring, and the first generation to introduced the high performance varient - The M3.

Later generations intoduced turbocharged engines, X-drive and driver assistant aids, the current generation-G20- which was launched in 2018 introduced a plug-in hybrid varient. 7 generation of the 3 series are present and more than 15 million units have been sold.
